About Me

I am 21 years old and was born in Fairhope, Alabama. I have lived in Kansas City since 1998.

I studied civil engineering at the University of Missouri for two years, and decided that it was not for me. I am currently changing course and taking a coding bootcamp at the University of Kansas. I have greatly enjoyed my time at the bootcamp so far and love taking on the challenges that coding presents. I have worked at Midland Metal Manufacturing during summers since I was in eigth grade. I like to paint in my free time and have done many commissions for my friends and family. I also enjoy spending time with my family, staying active, and watching sports.
